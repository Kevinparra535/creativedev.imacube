# Creative Tech Manifesto - Kevin Parra (KevCoder)

I am here to transform wild ideas into tangible experiences.

To build things that are remembered, that spark curiosity, and that are fun to explore, not just to use.

Technology is an instrument: the brush.

Stories and emotions are the canvas.

Creating is experimenting, and the web is my laboratory.

I do not just code: I think like a designer.

I like to create software that conveys sensation.

A failed prototype is better than an idea never built.

Animations must say something. If they do not, we failed.

Creativity and order can coexist to materialize ideas.

Sometimes the mission is simple: **pervert the web**.

Break patterns. Hack what is expected. Surprise.

The best way to learn is by doing: build, break, delete, try again.

Everything is part of the process.

Technology is the means, never the end.

Code is also art.

Bugs are teachers.

Curiosity is the spark.
